    answer: "Not initially. Small moisture pockets or light haze can appear during the first 24 to 72 hours of the curing process. This is normal and disappears as the film breathes.   - question: "Can seams be made invisible?"
    answer: "Mid-panel seams can be visible on complex body lines, especially on white cars. To deliver the best paint protection service in Dubai, we use custom-wrapped edge techniques to tuck the film inside panel gaps, making the edges virtually undetectable whether you choose a glossy or a matte PPF finish."
  - question: "Why is PPF more expensive than ceramic coating?"
    answer: "PPF is a thick, physical impact barrier that requires hours of custom plotting, stretching, and edge-tucking. It requires significantly more raw material and labor than a liquid coating. If you are looking for a reliable PPF shop near you, we prioritize flawless fitment, tucked edges, and longevity over rushing the job."
  - question: "Can I add ceramic coating over PPF?"
    answer: "Yes, and it is highly recommended. Adding ceramic over your film boosts gloss, adds hydrophobic protection, and prevents dirt buildup. It does not replace the rock-chip protection of the film, but combining them gives you the best paint protection."
  - question: "How do I care for PPF to avoid issues?"
    answer: "Hand-wash your car with pH-neutral soap, avoid automatic car washes with stiff brushes, and clean off bird droppings quickly. Do not pressure-wash near the edges during the first week while the film fully cures. Regular washing prevents the sand buildup that causes micro-scratches in the UAE environment."
