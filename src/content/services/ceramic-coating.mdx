faqs:
  - question: "How long does ceramic coating last in Dubai?"
    answer: "Professional coatings typically last 3–5 years, depending on product tier, prep quality, and maintenance. Harsh sun, sand, and outdoor parking shorten durability; regular washing and periodic decon help maintain performance."
  - question: "Will ceramic coating stop scratches?"
    answer: "Ceramic is not a substitute for PPF. It adds chemical/UV resistance and hydrophobicity and can reduce light marring, but it does not prevent rock chips or deeper scratches. For impact protection, choose PPF; many owners do PPF on high‑impact areas plus ceramic everywhere."
  - question: "I heard coatings can get water spots—true?"
    answer: "Yes. Hydrophobics reduce water contact time, but mineral‑rich water can still leave spots or etch if left to bake. Dry the car after washing, avoid sprinkler overspray, and address spots promptly with decon or light polishing if needed."
  - question: "Should I ceramic coat over PPF?"
    answer: "Yes. Ceramic on top of PPF improves gloss and makes the film easier to clean without affecting self‑healing. It does not replace PPF’s impact protection—they work best together."
